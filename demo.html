About 1.3 billion people live with a disability. When your site is accessible, you're not just avoiding legal risk — you're welcoming a large audience that would have otherwise quietly left.

What Insijam audits

  • Color contrast (WCAG 1.4.3)
  • Heading and landmark structure
  • Image alt text
  • Keyboard focus order
